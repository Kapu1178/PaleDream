Conceptualization is a character's ability to understand concepts, both common and foreign. A character with high conceptualization not only understands how something works, but why it works as well. They will be able to connect the dots in ways alien and unorthodox to others. They see art in the world where others see a blank canvas.

| Dots  | Description                                                                    |
| ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| •     | Novice: You had an active imagination as a child.                              |
| ••    | Practiced: A sketchbook of yours saw frequent use.                             |
| •••   | Competent: You frequently come up with unique solutions to problems presented. |
| ••••  | Expert: Any challenge is merely the sum of its parts.                          |
| ••••• | Master: You hold a degree in theoretical physics.                              |
