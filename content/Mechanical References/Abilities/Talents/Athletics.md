The Talent that covers basic athletic ability, as well as formal training in sports or other physical endeavors. Athletics covers all forms of running, jumping, swimming, throwing, and the like. A character with high Athletics might be a trained athlete, or gifted individual who spends lots of time training. This Talent does not cover simple motor actions like lifting, or other feats covered by other abilities such as boxing or fencing (covered by Physical Instrument and Melee respectively).
